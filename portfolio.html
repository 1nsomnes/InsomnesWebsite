Enlighten

Enlighten is one of the first games ever released by Nexela Studios. It is 2D platformer, where the main character explores a dark cave and attempts to find his way out. This all the while under constraint as the cave gets darker and darker. The player must hone into their dexterity and intuition in order to optimally maneuver through the cave and make it to the next level. It will be available on major mobile platforms.

Drilling for Treasure(Ludum Dare)

Drilling for Treasure is one of the projects that I worked on for a Ludum dare gamejam. My team and I were given 72 hours to come up with, design, and make a game with the theme "Running out of space." This was what we came up with, a fast paced dungeon crawler game in which faux ads would constantly take up the screen hence forcing the player to close them.
‍
You can play and view the submission at: https://nielisson.itch.io/drilling-for-treasure

Nexela Studio

Nexela Studio is a game development studio in curation by Insomnes. It currently is responsible for the creation of the game Enlightened and runs projects such as the "Game Development Community" which is a community of 200+ programmers.

Insomnes YouTube

The Insomnes YouTube channel aims for one goal. To create high quality programming content that is accessible to all. Through it I try to and continue to foster a community that promotes learning as well as entertainment. This becomes evident when looking at environments such as the "Game Development Discord," with over 200 members.
